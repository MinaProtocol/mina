Make `runtime_genesis_ledger` load hardfork accounts verbatim (identity conversion), matching the daemon's `Account.Hardfork.migrate_to_mesa`. The tool previously applied the Berkeley → Mesa vesting re-base (`Account.slot_reduction_update`) whenever a hardfork slot was derived from `--prefork-genesis-config`, which re-based already-Mesa timed accounts for a Mesa → Mesa fork and produced a genesis-ledger root that diverged from the daemon/automode root. The `slot_reduction_update` conversion, the hardfork-slot derivation and the now-purposeless `--prefork-genesis-config` flag are removed (release scripts updated accordingly). Fixes #18981.
