Fix Rosetta reporting corrupt locked and liquid balances for vesting accounts at historical blocks

Querying `/account/balance` with an explicit block identifier for an account with an active vesting schedule returned a `locked_balance` that had wrapped around to roughly 1.8×10^19 and a `liquid_balance` larger than the account's total balance. The total balance itself was always correct, and live balance queries were unaffected. The locked and liquid amounts are now computed correctly, so an exchange or explorer reading a vesting account at a past block sees consistent figures.
